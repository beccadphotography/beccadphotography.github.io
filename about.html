About Me

Hi, I'm Becca. I love photographing live music and nature. I also love attending concerts and festivals, spending time with friends, and traveling. I'm currently attending grad school for web design, so this website was fully created by me!

I currently shoot with a Canon Rebel SL1 camera, a Sigma 17-50mm f/2.8 lens, and a Canon 50mm f/1.8 lens.

You can find me on Instagram @beccadphotography.
